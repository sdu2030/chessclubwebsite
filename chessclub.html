Chess Club

Chess Club's purposes and goals:

The purpose and goals of chess club is to provide an environment for chess lovers to have friendly compeitions and to exchange knowledge about chess theory.

Chess Club's benefits and responsibilities:

The responsibilities of students in chess club is to uphold integrity and kindness to one another. Benefits of chess club include learning from one another and improving at the game through practice.

Chess Club's leadership skills:

Potential leadership opportunities in chess club include holding and participating in meetings as well as hosting and participating in tournaments.
